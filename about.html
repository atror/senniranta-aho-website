"I design objects that earn their place in a room — and in a life."

Senni Ranta-aho is a Helsinki-based furniture designer and spatial artist whose work explores the relationship between everyday objects and the people who live with them. Drawing on a background in industrial engineering, she creates pieces that are at once functional and quietly poetic.

Her practice spans limited-edition furniture, bespoke commissions, and interior interventions for private clients, exhibitions, and public spaces. Each work begins with function and material, shaped by the people who will use it, and ends somewhere between craft and sculpture.

Senni studied at Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture.
